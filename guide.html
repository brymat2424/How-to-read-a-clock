Step-by-Step Guide on How to Read a Clock

Home Examples Practice

  • Step 1: Understand the Clock Face

    The traditional analog clock face has 12 hours, marked by numbers 1 through 12. The clock has two hands: the shorter hand for the hour and the longer hand for minutes. Some clocks may have an additional small hand indicating seconds.

  • Step 2: Know the Hour Hand

    The hour hand points to the hour number. If it's between two numbers, it indicates that the time is between those hours.

  • Step 3: Know the Minute Hand

    The minute hand points to the minutes, with each number on the clock representing 5-minute intervals.

  • Step 4: Reading the Time

    First, look at the hour hand to get the hour. Then check the minute hand to determine the minutes.
